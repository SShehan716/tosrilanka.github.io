Sigiriya

Sigiriya is one of the most visited tourist destinations in Sri Lanka.The ancient palace and fortress complex has significant archaeological importance. It is located on a massive rocky plateau 370 meters above sea level. Sigiriya fortress complex includes remnants of a ruined palace surrounded by an extensive network of fortifications. The palace of Kasyapa was built on top of a huge stone lion, whose feet have survived up to today but the upper parts of the body were destroyed. Western wall of Sigiriya is almost entirely covered by frescoes depicting nude females. The Mirror wall is painted with inscriptions and poems written by the visitors.

Ella

Ella is a small village in the highlands of Sri Lanka which is filled with tea estates, mountains, waterfalls, and of course some good air to breathe. It is a dream place for many trekkers with some of the best hikes the Island provides. If you are looking for a place to unwind from your busy lifestyle and spend some peaceful, relaxed time with nature, this is the place.

Temple of the Tooth

The Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic is a world-renowned place of worship, where the left tooth of Gautama Buddha is enshrined. The temple which is venerated by thousands of local & foreign devotees and tourists daily was named a world heritage by UNESCO in 1988. The architecture is of unique Kandyan architectural style with a combination of the unique style used to build "Dalada Mandira".

Hikkaduwa Beach

Surfing in the Hikkaduwa area is fairly well known and gets the best surfing during its dry season, which is from November to March. Scuba diving and diving in clear waters are a major past-time along this stretch. There are more than four different shipwrecks for diving enthusiasts to explore as well as diving shops offering PADI courses and equipment.

x

Galle Fort

Galle Fort is a Portuguese fortress that was built in 1588 at the Bay of Galle on the southwestern coast of Sri Lanka. UNESCO declared Galle Dutch Fort as a World Heritage in 1988. The most prominent buildings in the fort complex were the Commandant's residence, the arsenal, and the gun house.
